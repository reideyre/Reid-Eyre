courses and training

  • FIRST AID CERTIFICATIONS
    from basic to advanced - i teach it all

    Standard and Emergency First Aid courses offer first aid and cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) skills for those who need training due to work requirements or want more knowledge to respond to emergencies at home. Each course includes the latest first aid and CPR guidelines from the Canadian Red Cross.  

    More specific First Aid courses are also available, including Wilderness & Remote First Aid, Marine First Aid, Babysitting courses, and more. To learn about which course best suits your needs, reach out to me and I'd be happy to walk you through the best options for you.

  • aquatic FITNESS PROGRAMS
    custom training just for you

    Swimming is a great workout for your entire body. Whether you're a strong swimmer or just starting out, you have so much to gain by taking some time in the water. Whatever your goals are- training for a triathlon, practicing for that next certification, or just wanting to get more active- I'll build a program that works just for you, and walk you through it every step of the way.

  • SWIMMING PROGRAMS
    KIDS TO ADULTS, BEGINNERS TO VETERANS

    Swimming is a lifeskill, especially for those of us in Canada with so much open water. Some learn as kids, but some don't get that opportunity. With over seven years working in aquatics, and qualifications to teach all three federally-recognized curriculums in Canada, I'm ready to take you or your child to the level that makes you comfortable. From the curriculum to custom programs, I'll do whatever it takes to get you there at a pace that works for you.

  • RESPECT EDUCATION
    CREATING A SAFER WORLD FOR EVERYONE

    I want to make the world safer in any way that I can, so I participate in the Canadian Red Cross RespectED program. RespectED provides you with education to create a safer world around you. Whether your goal is to increase your own knowledge or to find education options for your students, clients or personnel, let me provide you with the knowledge and skills you need now.

    RespectED courses include Bullying Prevention, training for how parents can protect their children from sexual abuse, how organizations can help protect children/youth from abuse or bullying, workplace bullying and violence, training for schools, and plenty more. If you or someone you know is interested in these remarkable, short courses- reach out to me. I'd love to tell you more about them.
